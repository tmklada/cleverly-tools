## Is It Legal to Download YouTube Videos?

This is a common question. Here's the honest answer:

- YouTube's Terms of Service generally prohibit downloading unless you use YouTube Premium
- However, downloading for **personal offline viewing** is widely practiced and rarely enforced
- Downloading copyrighted music videos or movies for redistribution is illegal
- **Creative Commons** licensed videos can be freely downloaded and reused

Use your judgment: saving a tutorial to watch without internet is very different from uploading someone else's video to your own channel.
