## Tips for Working with PDFs Online

- **Process sensitive documents carefully**: For highly confidential files (legal contracts, medical records, financial documents), choose tools that process files locally in your browser rather than uploading to servers.
- **Compress before merging**: If you are combining large PDFs, compress each file first to keep the final merged document manageable.
- **Use PDF for final versions only**: Work in editable formats (Word, Google Docs) while making changes, then export to PDF when the document is finalized.
- **Check the result before sharing**: Always open and review the processed PDF before sending it to confirm everything looks correct.
