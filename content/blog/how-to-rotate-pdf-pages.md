## Why PDF Pages Get Rotated Wrong

Before diving into how to fix it, here is why this happens in the first place:

- **Scanning**: When you place a document on a scanner sideways, the resulting PDF comes out rotated
- **Mobile photos**: Photos taken in portrait mode but saved as landscape PDFs end up sideways
- **Merged PDFs**: When you combine files from different sources, some pages may arrive at the wrong angle
- **Camera scans**: Using your phone camera to scan documents often produces inconsistent orientations

Whatever the reason, the fix takes under a minute.

## Tips for Rotating PDFs

- **Rotate before merging**: If you are combining several PDFs, fix the rotation on each file first. This saves time compared to correcting pages after merging.
- **Check all pages**: Scroll through your PDF preview to make sure every page looks correct before downloading.
- **180° for upside-down pages**: If a page appears completely inverted, choose 180° instead of rotating it twice.
- **Protect your privacy**: For sensitive documents like contracts or medical records, use a browser-based tool that does not upload files to a server.
