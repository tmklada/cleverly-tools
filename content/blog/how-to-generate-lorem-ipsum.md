## When Do You Need Placeholder Text?

Placeholder text is useful in more situations than you might think:

### Website and App Design
When designing a website or app, you need to see how text-heavy sections will look before the real copy is written. Lorem ipsum fills in blog posts, product descriptions, testimonials, and more.

### Wireframes and Prototypes
Wireframes show the structure of a page without the final visuals. Using lorem ipsum in wireframes prevents clients or stakeholders from getting distracted by the words instead of the layout.

### Print Design
Brochures, flyers, posters, and books all need to be laid out before the content is finalized. Placeholder text lets designers work ahead.

### Presentations and Mockups
When showing a concept to a client, placeholder text keeps the focus on the design — not unfinished copy that might raise questions.

### Testing Text Rendering
Developers use lorem ipsum to test how fonts, line spacing, and text containers behave across different screen sizes and environments.

## The History of Lorem Ipsum

The original passage from Cicero reads: *"Neque porro quisquam est, qui dolorem ipsum quia dolor sit amet..."* — "Nor is there anyone who loves grief itself because it is grief..."

The lorem ipsum version used in design since the 1960s was popularized by Letraset, a company that made transfer type sheets used by designers. When desktop publishing became common in the 1980s, programs like Aldus PageMaker included lorem ipsum as default placeholder text — and it stuck.

Today, it's the universal language of "this text isn't real yet."

## Tips for Using Placeholder Text Professionally

- **Don't present placeholder text in final deliverables.** Always replace lorem ipsum before handing off to a client.
- **Use appropriate lengths.** A hero headline placeholder shouldn't be a full paragraph.
- **Match the structure.** If the real content will be two short sentences, use two short sentences of lorem ipsum — not five long ones.
- **Communicate clearly.** When sharing mockups, make sure stakeholders know the text is placeholder so they don't try to proofread it.
