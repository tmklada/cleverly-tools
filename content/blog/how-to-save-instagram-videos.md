## Is Downloading Instagram Videos Legal?

This is the practical answer:

- **Personal use** — Saving a video to watch offline is generally fine
- **Sharing with credit** — Reposting with the original creator tagged is usually tolerated
- **Commercial use** — Using someone else's Instagram video in ads or paid content is not legal without permission
- **Monetized reposting** — Building a channel around reposting others' content can get your account banned and may violate copyright

When in doubt, message the creator and ask for permission. Most will say yes.
