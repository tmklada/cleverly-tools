# How to Download Twitter/X Videos for Free (2026 Guide)

Twitter (now rebranded as X) is full of valuable video content — news clips, tutorials, sports highlights, funny moments, and more. The platform doesn't have a built-in download button, but our [Twitter Video Downloader](/tools/twitter-video-downloader) lets you save any public video in seconds, for free.

## Why Twitter Doesn't Have a Download Button

X/Twitter removed or never widely implemented a native download feature because video content drives users back to the platform to watch. When you download a video, you consume it offline and don't return to Twitter — which means fewer ads shown and less engagement on the platform.

Third-party tools fill this gap. They work by extracting the video file from the public tweet URL and giving you a direct download link.

## How to Download a Twitter/X Video — Step by Step

### Step 1: Find the Tweet with the Video
Open Twitter/X and find the video you want to download. It can be a regular tweet video, a Retweet, or a quote tweet.

### Step 2: Copy the Tweet URL
- On **desktop**: Click the share icon on the tweet → "Copy link" (or click the three dots → Copy link to tweet)
- On **mobile**: Tap the share icon → "Copy link"

The URL will look like: `https://twitter.com/username/status/1234567890` or `https://x.com/username/status/1234567890`

### Step 3: Paste into the Downloader
Go to our [Twitter Video Downloader](/tools/twitter-video-downloader) and paste the URL into the input field.

### Step 4: Choose Your Quality
Click **Download** and the tool will show you available quality options — typically:
- **High Quality (HD)** — best resolution available
- **Medium Quality** — smaller file size
- **Low Quality** — fastest download, smallest file

### Step 5: Download the Video
Click on the quality you want and the video will download to your device.

## Can You Download Twitter Spaces or Live Streams?

Twitter Spaces (audio) and live video streams are different from regular tweet videos. They are not downloadable through standard video downloaders while they're live. Recordings of Spaces, if made available by the host, may be accessible after the broadcast ends.

## Is It Legal to Download Twitter Videos?

This is an important question. Here's a straightforward breakdown:

- **Personal use** — Downloading a video for your own offline viewing is generally considered acceptable.
- **Sharing with attribution** — If you share downloaded content, crediting the original creator is the right thing to do.
- **Commercial use** — Using someone else's video for commercial purposes without permission is a copyright violation.
- **Re-uploading** — Uploading someone else's Twitter video to your own channel or account without permission can infringe on copyright.

Always respect the original creator's rights. If you're unsure, ask for permission.

## Tips for Downloading Twitter Videos

- **Always start from the original tweet.** If the video appears in a Retweet, go to the original tweet for the best quality options.
- **Check the resolution before downloading.** Some older videos or GIF-style clips are only available in lower resolutions.
- **Downloaded GIFs from Twitter come as MP4.** Twitter converts all GIFs to video format (MP4) internally. This is normal.
- **Muted videos download with the mute.** If a video is auto-muted on Twitter, the downloaded file may or may not have audio depending on the original upload.
