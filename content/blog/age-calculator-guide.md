## How Leap Years Affect Age Calculation

A standard year has 365 days. A leap year has 366. Leap years occur every 4 years (with some exceptions). Over a lifetime, those extra days add up.

If your birthday is February 29, you only have a "real" birthday every 4 years — technically making you about 7–8 "birthday years" old at age 28–32. Our calculator handles this correctly and still gives you your precise age.
